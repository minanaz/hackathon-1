Gojo Satoru

Strongest. Limitless. Just call me Satoru.

Wanna know about me?

One of the main protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. A special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. The pride of the Gojo Family. The first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years.

Skills

Immense Strength

Satoru possesses tremendous physical strength to fight against powerful curses without a problem. He could casually rip apart transfigured humans, tearing them apart limb-by-limb.

Immense Speed & Reflexes

Satoru is an extremely fast fighter, capable of moving faster than the eye could perceive. Satoru can also eliminate a thousand transfigured humans in five minutes.

Tactical Intellect

Gojo is quite tactical and capable of figuring out his opponent's plan with minimum information. He has also shown to be extremely adaptable to any enemy he has ever faced so far, knowing precisely what to do to counter their techniques and find ways to defeat them.
